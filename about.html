Who am I?

With a degree focus on Human-Computer Interaction, I am passionate about the interplay between people and digital interfaces.

Looking ahead, I aim to delve deeper into this fusion by pursuing a Masters in Media & Information by 2025. I'm excited by the prospect to forever be able to design polished and well-intentioned interfaces.
